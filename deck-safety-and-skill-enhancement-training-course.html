DECK SAFETY AND SKILL ENHANCEMENT TRAINING COURSE (SSTD)

    Course Duration :- 1 Week


Deck Safety And Skill Enhancement Training Course
  1. Personal Safety and Safe working Practices On Board Vessel
  2. Dealing with various Emergencies On Board Vessel
  3. Communication Practice to be followed On Board Vessel
  4. Familiarization of enclosed Space Entry Permit, Hot Working Permit and Working Aloft Permit
Safety Precaution to be taken during :
  1. Handling Different Types of Hand Tools
  2. Overhauling of Engine Room Machineries and Equiprnents such as Pumps and Valves
  3. Seaman Ship practice such as Pilot Ladder Rigging, Wire Rope Splicing, Mooring Rope Slicing etc
  4. Handling Lifting Equipments such as Chain Block, Pulleys, Cranes etc
  5. Handling Deck Hydraulic and Pneumatic Equipment
  6. Securing Heavy Objects in Engine Room and On Deck
  7. Electric Arc Welding and Gas Cutting Operations
