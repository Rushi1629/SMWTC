About us

We are team of experienced Chief Engineer and skilled fitters having vast experience of Marine Industry. Here to share our experience and knowledge to the new enthusiasts who are willing to pursue their carrier in Marine Industry as Marine Fitter.

Our Mission

Our mission is to uphold the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and excellence in the maritime sector. We strive to provide comprehensive and practical training programs that meet international standards, promote professional development, and encourage sustainable maritime practices.

Experienced Instructors

Our instructors are seasoned maritime professionals with extensive industry experience. They bring real-world knowledge and practical expertise to the classroom, ensuring that our trainees receive the best possible education. Our instructors also stay current with the latest developments in maritime technology and practices, providing up-to-date training.

Extensive Training Programs

We offer a wide array of training programs designed to meet the needs of both new and experienced maritime professionals. Our programs include:

Basic Safety Training: Essential safety procedures and emergency response techniques.
Advanced Navigation: Mastery of modern navigation systems and techniques.
Engine Room Management: Detailed instruction on the operation and maintenance of marine engines and systems.
Maritime Security: Comprehensive strategies and procedures to ensure the security of vessels and ports.
Environmental Protection: Training on sustainable practices and compliance with environmental regulations.

Cutting-Edge Facilities

Our facilities are equipped with the latest technology to provide an immersive and effective learning experience:

Workshops: Fully equipped workshops for hands-on training in mechanical and electrical systems.
Classrooms: Modern classrooms outfitted with the latest educational technology.

Why Choose SMTC&MW?

Excellence in Education: Our programs are designed to prepare you for a successful career in the maritime industry.
Global Standards: We adhere to international standards, making our training programs globally recognized and respected.
Practical Experience: Gain hands-on experience with our state-of-the-art facilities and training vessels.
Sustainable Practices: Learn and implement environmentally responsible practices to ensure a sustainable future for the maritime industry.
Supportive Environment: Our team is dedicated to supporting your professional growth and development at every step.
