Engine Fitter Workshop Skill Upgradation Course

    Course Duration :- 9 Week


Engine Fitter Workshop Skill Upgradation Course
  • Personal safety and safe working practices on board vessels
  • Correct use of Measuring Instruments like, Vernier Calliper, inside and outside Micrometer and Dial Gauges.
  • Principle and Application of Various types of Valves such as Gate Valve, Globe Valve, Butterfly Valve, Pressure Reducing Valve and Ball Valve etc.
  • Overhauling of Centrifugal Pump, Gear Pump, Vane Pump, Screw Pump and Wilden Pump.
  • Overhauling of Centrifugal Purifier, Main Air Compressor and Auxiliary Engine Turbocharger and Electric Motor.
  • Overhauling of Auxiliary Engine Cylinder Head, Piston Connecting Rod Assembly, Fuel Pump, and Fuel Enjector Calibration of Cylinder Liner and Piston.
  • Electric Arc Welding in 1G, 2G, 3G, 4G, 5G & 6G, Positions, Gas cutting of Flanges, Gas Welding of Copper Pipe, Cast Iron Welding.
  • Pipe Fabrication with Elbow, Fabrication of Cement Box.
  • Use of Flaring Tools Ferruling on Copper Pipe Thread Extractors.
  • Lathe Machine Skills Upgradation in Turning, Facing, V and Square Thread Cutting and Grooving etc.
